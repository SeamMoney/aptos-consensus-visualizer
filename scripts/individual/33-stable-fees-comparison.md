## Key Points
- Solana March 2024: 100,000x fee spike during memecoin mania
- Aptos: Flat fees regardless of demand
- Fee auctions vs governance-set fees
- Arrival-based ordering vs highest-bidder-wins
- Aptos responds to demand with throughput, not price increases
- Regular users never priced out
